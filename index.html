Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start meditating?

Start with just 5-10 minutes each day. Find a quiet space, focus on your breathing, and let thoughts come and go.

What is the best time to meditate?

The best time to meditate is when you can do so without distractions, whether in the morning or before bed.
